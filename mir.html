International Perspective

As per the world bank pink sheet Rapeseed oil prices in Mar 2024 averaged $ 1029/MT which was higher by 7% compared to Feb 2024 when prices were $962/MT and 2% lower when compared to Mar 2023 when prices were $1052.7/MT.

As per USDA, Rapeseed oilseed production globally has fallen by 1%. It was 88.83 MMT in 2022-23 and is estimated at 88.07 MMT for 2023-24.

As per USDA, Soyabean oilseed is the most produced oilseed globally and its production increased by 5% year on year from 378 MMT to 396.85 MMT.

As per USDA, Palm production slightly increased year on year from 20.07 MMT in 2022-23 to 20.71 MMT in 2023-24

As per USDA, Sunflower oilseed production slightly decreased year on year from 55.08 MMT in 2022-23 to 54.91 MMT in 2023-24.

As per USDA, Total oilseed production globally has increased year on year by 3%. It was 637.28 MMT in 2022-23 and was 658.68 MMT in 2023-24.

INFLATION:

WPI inflation: Monthly average retail prices of Rice as on Feb 2024 was Rs. 4513.32 per quintal. Compared to same month last year Feb 2023 prices are higher by 14.98%.

CPI inflation: Usually, CPI inflation in rice tends to be at its lowest level in Oct, Nov & Dec, which coincides with the kharif crop’s harvest. It rises thereafter, and arrival of rabi & summer crop tends to moderate the rising trend around Jul or Aug & CPI inflation peaks in Sep, right before the onset of the next kharif crop. Inflation in the Consumer Price Index in Dec 2023 was 12.33% which is higher than 10.49% reported in the same month last year and also higher than 11.81% reported in previous month.
